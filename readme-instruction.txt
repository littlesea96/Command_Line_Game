**You need to take items, both potion and weapon, to your bag before use

Commands:
info    print out the player Hp, Exp, all items in bag
        print out all monsters and items in the room

go <direction>    move player to next room in specific direction
use <potionName>    use specific potion which has specific hp value to increase player hp
take <itemName>   take item, including potion and weapon, in the room into player bag
attackWith <weaponName>   attack a monster within a current room with specific weapon
quit    quit the game
help    print all available commands
